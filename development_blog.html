2016.11.19 - Login Service Complete

— by Wade Wooldridge

The login_service is complete. I have created a proto_login_controller that can serve as a template for creating the real login_controller when we are ready. I have also created a proto_login_page.html that can serve as a template for the real login page; it also provides a test harness for the login_service, so I can make sure it is working. 2016.11.16 - Simplification and the Soft Hook

— by Wade Wooldridge

After a long meeting with Eric and then separately, the app has morphed significantly. Based primarily on Eric's experience, we have to shoot for a much simpler first-user experience: only a few clicks required to get some response from the server. Dan, Junie, and I are working through the new-user flows to make this as painless as possible for the user. Of course that means we are jettisoning most of the filters, becuase the more we ask, the more chance we have where the user will just say, "to hell with it."
